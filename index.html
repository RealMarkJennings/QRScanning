How this demo works

Each table has a printed QR code. A guest scans it, the menu opens on their phone already tagged to that table, they order, and the order appears live on the Manager Dashboard — no waiter, no waiting. This build has no backend or real payments; orders are stored in your browser so you can try the whole flow end to end.

Try it: tap a table below (or scan its QR with your phone) to open the menu and place an order, then open the Manager Dashboard to watch it come through. For the QR to work on a phone, host these files (e.g. GitHub Pages) and scan from there.
